A:	Wash your hands with warm water. 

Q:	Do I need to dry my hands?/What do I dry my hands with?
A:	Please use a clean paper towel to dry your hands.

Q:	Where should I set the paper towel and trash bag?

A:	You will pretend to place your old dressing in the trash bag at a later step.

A:	Take your bandage off while you are showering. It is more comfortable to remove a wet dressing.

Q:	How should I wash my wound?
A:	Wash your wound gently with soap and water.

Q:	What do I wash my wound with? 
A:	Please use your clean hands to wash your wound with soap and water.

A:	Wash your wound for thirty seconds.

Q:  Where do I put the bandage?
A:	Place the bandage in the trash bag.

----------------------------------------

Q:	What do I dry the wound with?
A:	Dry your wound with a clean paper towel.

Q:	Can I use a paper towel to dry my wound?
A:	You may use a paper towel to dry your wound if the paper towel is clean.

Q:	How dry does the wound need to be? 
A:	Please remove visible drops of water.

----------------------------------------

P:  Cut the non-stick dressing then open the Vaseline. 

Q:	How should I cut the dressing?/What size do I cut the dressing?
A:	Cut the dressing to a size that will cover the wound.

Q:	Which of the supplies is the nonstick dressing?
A:	The nonstick dressing is in a rectangular package with the word “Telfa” written on it.

----------------------------------------

Q:	Can I use my finger?
A:	Please use the supplied Q tips. Do not use your finger. 

Q:	How thick of a layer/How much Vaseline? 
A:	Spread a quarter inch layer. 

Q:	How should I spread the Vaseline?
A:	Spread a quarter inch layer of Vaseline like you are icing a cake.

----------------------------------------

A:	Use as many strips of tape as you need to secure the dressing.

Q:	How do I tape the dressing on?
A:	Apply multiple strips of tape until the dressing is secure.
